A progressive, six-level series equipping learners from primary school to professional research with the knowledge, critical thinking, and ethical awareness to understand, evaluate, and use artificial intelligence responsibly.

L2

Critical Thinking AI Ethics Research Skills Multilingual AI Academic Integrity Prompt Engineering
💡
Flexible Enrolment
Each level is available as a standalone course and can be opted for independently. Participants may enrol in whichever level best matches their context and needs. All courses are delivered online.

Certificate of Completion
A certificate of completion is awarded at the end of every level, recognising the participant's achievement upon successfully completing the course requirements.
Wordetica Human-Centered AI Literacy Training Series
01 / 07
Level 1 · Primary Education
Human-Centered AI:
Foundations for Primary Education
Target audience: Learners aged 6–11
✓ Standalone course available
🎉 Certificate of completion
Programme Vision
"Helping young learners understand what AI is, where it appears in their daily lives, and how to engage with it safely, creatively, and responsibly."
Learning Objectives
  • Understand what AI is in simple, accessible terms
  • Recognise AI systems encountered in everyday life
  • Use AI tools safely and responsibly
  • Develop foundational digital citizenship skills
  • Explore creativity and learning through AI-supported activities
Modules
  • M1
    What Is AI?
    Machines that learn · AI vs humans · What AI can and cannot do
  • M2
    AI in Everyday Life
    Smart assistants · Recommendation systems · Games & entertainment
  • M3
    Safe and Responsible AI Use
    Privacy & personal information · Safe online behaviour · Trustworthy information
  • M4
    Creativity and Learning with AI
    Storytelling · Drawing & image generation · Brainstorming · Learning support
  • M5
    Digital Citizenship
    Respectful communication · Digital footprints · Healthy technology habits
Applications
AI-assisted storytelling exercises Creative drawing & image generation tasks Digital safety role-play activities
Wordetica Human-Centered AI Literacy Training Series
02 / 07
Level 2 · Secondary Education
Human-Centered AI:
Foundations for Secondary Education
Target audience: Learners aged 12–15
✓ Standalone course available
🎉 Certificate of completion
Programme Vision
"Building critical thinking, media literacy, and ethical awareness so young people can navigate an AI-saturated information environment with confidence and discernment."
Learning Objectives
  • Understand how AI systems work at a foundational level
  • Develop critical thinking about AI-generated content
  • Identify misinformation, deepfakes, and manipulation
  • Apply ethical principles when using AI
  • Begin communicating effectively with AI systems
Modules
  • M1
    Understanding AI Systems
    How AI learns · Training & prediction · Algorithms · Limitations
  • M2
    Critical Thinking and AI
    Evaluating AI information · Fact-checking · Detecting hallucinations · Human oversight
  • M3
    AI and Media Literacy
    Deepfakes · Synthetic media · Misinformation · Social media algorithms
  • M4
    Ethics, Fairness, and Bias
    Sources of bias · Fairness in AI · Representation & inclusion · Ethical decision-making
  • M5
    Introduction to Prompting
    Asking effective questions · Clear instructions · Evaluating & improving AI responses
Applications
Deepfake and misinformation detection exercises Bias audit of AI-generated content Hands-on prompt design and evaluation tasks

Level 5 · Academic Research Specialisation
Human-Centered AI: Academic Writing
and Research Communication
Target audience: Advanced undergraduates, postgraduates, researchers & academics
✓ Standalone course available
🎉 Certificate of completion
Programme Vision
"Enabling researchers and academics to integrate AI into rigorous scholarly workflows - from literature discovery to publication ethics - while maintaining the methodological integrity and intellectual ownership their work demands."
Learning Objectives
  • Integrate AI into academic writing workflows responsibly
  • Conduct systematic literature exploration with AI support
  • Improve research communication practices for diverse audiences
  • Critically evaluate AI-assisted outputs at every research stage
  • Maintain academic integrity and ethical disclosure standards
Modules
  • M1
    AI-Assisted Academic Writing
    Idea development · Outlining · Drafting support · Revision strategies
  • M2
    Literature Reviews and Knowledge Discovery
    Search strategies · AI-powered research tools · Evidence synthesis · Gap identification
  • M3
    AI-Supported Research Workflows
    Research planning · Data organisation · Project management · Knowledge management
  • M4
    Scholarly Communication
    Academic style · Audience adaptation · Research dissemination · Public communication
  • M5
    Publication Ethics and Responsible AI
    Authorship · Transparency · Citation practices · Ethical disclosure
Applications
AI-assisted systematic literature review Research communication and dissemination portfolio Authorship and AI use statement

    Localisation workflows · Cultural adaptation · Accessibility · Global communication

    Ethics, Bias, and the Future of Language Technologies
    Linguistic bias · Low-resource languages · Inclusion & accessibility · Future directions
